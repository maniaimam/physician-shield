OUR SERVICES

Physician Shield is your dedicated partner for optimizing your practice’s revenue cycle. Our full suite of services is designed to reduce administrative burdens, boost your revenue, and ensure that your practice runs smoothly and profitably. We focus on the details so you can focus on delivering excellent care.

Patient Registration and Scheduling

We streamline patient intake and scheduling to ensure smooth, hassle-free appointments. Our service improves patient satisfaction and reduces no-shows, leading to a more productive practice.
Note: This service is available at an additional cost.

Insurance Verification and Eligibility Checks

Say goodbye to unexpected claim denials. We proactively verify insurance coverage and patient eligibility before appointments, reducing the risk of denied claims and helping you maintain a predictable revenue stream.

Medical Coding and Charge Entry

Our certified coders ensure accurate and compliant medical coding, so you never leave revenue on the table. We guarantee that your services are properly documented, coded, and entered promptly, ensuring faster reimbursements.
Note: Additional charges apply for this service

Claims Submission and Management

Avoid costly delays with our comprehensive claims submission process. We handle everything from initial submission to real-time tracking and follow-up, ensuring that claims are processed accurately and quickly for maximum reimbursement.

Payment Posting and Reconciliation

We accurately post payments and reconcile your accounts, ensuring that every payment received is matched to the correct claim. Our meticulous attention to detail helps identify and resolve discrepancies, ensuring your financials are always in balance.

Denial Management and Appeals

When claims are denied, your revenue takes a hit. We proactively manage denials, investigating the root cause and filing appeals to recover lost revenue. Our experts work diligently to resolve claim issues quickly and minimize financial impact.

Accounts Receivable (AR) Follow-up

Don’t let unpaid claims drag down your cash flow. Our AR follow-up team aggressively pursues overdue claims, improving collections and shortening the time to payment. We ensure that no claim falls through the cracks.

Patient Billing and Collections

Patient balances can be tricky, but we take the stress off your practice by handling patient billing and collections. From generating statements to following up on outstanding balances, we ensure a patient-friendly process that protects your relationship while improving cash flow.

Reporting and Analytics

Gain valuable insights into your practice’s financial health with our comprehensive reporting and analytics. We provide you with detailed, actionable data on KPIs such as claim success rates, collections, and AR aging. Our reports empower you to make informed decisions and optimize your revenue cycle.

Credentialing and Provider Enrollment

Navigating the complex credentialing process is crucial for maintaining your practice’s revenue stream. Our team handles the entire credentialing and payer enrollment process, ensuring that your providers are set up to bill payers quickly and correctly.
Note: Additional charges apply for this service.

Audit and Quality Assurance

Compliance is key in today’s healthcare landscape. Our in-depth audit and quality assurance services ensure that your practice is compliant with regulatory standards. We regularly review your processes to identify any potential risks or areas for improvement, safeguarding your practice from legal or financial penalties.
